Cabin Crew Operations (Aviation English)

Operational Excellence:

This manual covers the three pillars of Cabin Crew operations: Safety, Medical, and Service. It is designed to be encyclopedic in scope but practical in application.

Online Simulation Mode:

  • Camera Demos: Students stand back from the camera to perform Safety Demonstrations.
  • Audio Drills: Use the 'Mute' button to simulate Intercom announcements (PA).
  • Breakout Cabins: Small groups simulate 'Galley' and 'Cabin' scenarios.
