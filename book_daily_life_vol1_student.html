Daily Life English: Volume 1 (A2)

Course Overview:

Level A2 moves beyond survival. This is Social English. Students learn to express opinions, tell stories, and manage relationships.

Online Teaching Strategy:

  • Breakout Rooms: Essential for social simulations. "Go and gossip for 5 minutes."
  • Screen Sharing Docs: Filling out forms together (e.g. Travel Visa).
  • Chat Fiction: Writing a story line-by-line in the chat box.

1. Advanced Greetings

Zoom Warm Up
Speed Update:
30 seconds. Tell the class everything you did this week. 'Go!'
Theory & Vocab
1. Core Vocabulary:
How have you been?Long time no seeWhat's new?Can't complainCatch upStrangerLifeBusySame oldFantasticAwfulSurviveGreetingHugShake hands

2. Concept: 1. Present Perfect (Recent News):
I have been busy.
What have you done recently?
2. Echo Questions:
'I went to Paris.' 'Did you?' (Showing interest).
Controlled Practice
Casual Response Drill:
Teacher: 'How are you?' Student: 'Not bad, you?' (Avoid 'I am fine thank you').
Online Simulation
Street Encounter:
Scenario: You see an old friend on the street.
'Hey! Is that you?'
'Oh my god, it has been years!'
'Let's get coffee.'
