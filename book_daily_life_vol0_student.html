Daily Life English: Volume 0 (A1)

Course Overview:

This course is the absolute foundation of English. It is designed for True Beginners. The focus is on survival skills, basic phonics, and immediate "Can-Do" statements.

Online Teaching Strategy:

  • Total Physical Response (TPR) via Camera: "Touch your nose!" "Hold up a pen!" Use the webcam actively.
  • Mute/Unmute Drills: Rapid-fire pronunciation checks. "Unmute and say: APPLE."
  • Visual Anchors: Always use Screen Share for images. Do not rely on text alone for this level.

1. The Alphabet & Phonics

Zoom Warm Up
Mouth Shape Challenge (Camera):
Teacher mimes a letter (no sound). Students guess: 'Is it O?' 'Is it M?'
Theory & Vocab
1. Core Vocabulary:
A (Apple)B (Ball)C (Cat)D (Dog)E (Egg)F (Fish)G (Goat)H (Hat)I (Ice)J (Jam)VowelConsonantSoundLetterSpell

2. Concept: 1. The Vowels (A, E, I, O, U):
These are the open sounds. Your mouth is open.
2. The Consonants (B, C, D...):
These sounds are blocked by lips or tongue.
Controlled Practice
The Spelling Race (Chat):
Teacher says: 'DOG'. Students type: 'D-O-G'. Fastest typer wins.
Online Simulation
Hotel Check-in (Spelling):
Roleplay: Student arrives at hotel.
Receptionist: 'What is your name?'
Guest: 'Smith. S-M-I-T-H.'

3. Numbers 0-100

Theory & Vocab
