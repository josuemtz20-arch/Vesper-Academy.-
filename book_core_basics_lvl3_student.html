Lesson 20 - Level 3 Final Review (A2 Exit)
VOCABULARY
• Tenses · verb forms that show time
• Modal · helper verb: can, should, must
• Conditional · an if-sentence: condition + result
• Passive · the focus is the action, not the doer: 'It was built'
• Relative clause · extra info with who/which/that
• Reported speech · retelling what someone said: 'She said that...'
• Phrasal verb · verb + particle with new meaning (give up)
• Opinion · what someone believes — not provable
• Describe · to say what something is like
• Narrate · to tell a story in order
• Negotiate · to discuss until you agree on a price
• Justify · to give reasons for your opinion
• Expand · to make longer with more detail
• Connect · to join ideas together
• Fluency · speaking smoothly without long pauses
